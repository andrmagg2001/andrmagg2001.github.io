Currently I'm working on a generative AI system for real-time music accompaniment conditioned on guitar performances — a project at the boundary of audio processing and transformer architectures, under preparation for publication.

Outside academia, I've shipped production code for the Galileo satellite navigation program, built embedded systems for industrial automation, and developed cloud-native full-stack applications.

03 — Research

Papers &
Publications.

On-going · Under Review
MASUC: Multi-Agent Socialized Unlearning for Classification
A multi-agent framework for efficient, targeted Machine Unlearning. ResNet-18 students "forget" specific training classes guided by a society of teacher models via Reciprocal Altruism and Energy Alignment — without retraining from scratch.
A. Maggiore, A. Radu — 2025
On-going · MSc Thesis
Conditional Music Generation — Real-Time AI Accompaniment
Neural pipeline that analyses live guitar performances (chord progression, tempo, style) and generates multi-track accompaniments (bass, drums, keys) using CNNs and Transformer architectures. Under preparation for publication.
A. Maggiore — Sapienza, 2025
